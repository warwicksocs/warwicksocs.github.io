Warwick Student Cinema

That's right, campus has a cinema! With 5 films a week, including brand-new blockbusters and old classics, very cheap tickets (3 pounds for members!), and fun Ikea socials, this society is made for everyone!

Check their website for a timetable, and more information about getting involved if you want FREE FILMS.

Warwick Maths Society

Not only targetted to Maths student, this society offers a huge range of things: boardgame and pizza nights, talks by mathematicians, socials to POP!, hoodies, academic help... There's always something going on!

They're free to join, so why not grab a membership?
